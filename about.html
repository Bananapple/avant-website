We are operators, we roll up our sleeves, we have skin in the game.

The truth is, the business world is pretty unforgiving. We are deeply familiar with the ups and downs of comapny-building and leverage our operational experience to help our companies succeed. The need for support varies over time and across our portfolio, but we tend to see ourselves almost as co-founders of the businesses we invest in, going beyond being a management sounding board.

We adapt our approach to set businesses up for the long term

Every business is unique and faces its own set of opportunities and challenges. Our investing style adapts to the needs of the businesses and teams we back, always with a goal to set up the business for long term success. Our investments have ranged from angel and venture deals to buy-outs, restructuring and turn-arounds.

Creating winning teams is core to our playbook

You've heard it before, the most important thing is the team behind the idea. We couldn't agree more, but if it only were this simple. Fundamentally speaking, building a company is about building a high performing team. The ups and downs, the bumps in the road, the fires that need to be put out, all involve dealing with people. That's exaclty what we love doing.
